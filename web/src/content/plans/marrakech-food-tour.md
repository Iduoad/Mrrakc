Discover the vibrant flavors of Marrakech on this guided food tour. From the bustling Jemaa el-Fnaa square to hidden gems in the Medina, you'll taste authentic Moroccan dishes and learn about the city's rich culinary history.

### What to Expect

*   **Authentic Flavors**: Sample traditional tagines, fresh pastries, and refreshing mint tea.
*   **Local Insights**: Learn about the ingredients and cooking techniques from local experts.
*   **Hidden Gems**: Explore the winding streets of the Medina and discover places you wouldn't find on your own.

Prepare your taste buds for an unforgettable adventure!
