Embark on a challenging yet rewarding trek through the High Atlas Mountains. This multi-day journey takes you through stunning landscapes, traditional Berber villages, and up to the refuge at the base of Mount Toubkal, North Africa's highest peak.

## Highlights

- **Scenic Views:** Experience breathtaking panoramas of the Atlas range.
- **Cultural Immersion:** Walk through local villages and meet the friendly Berber people.
- **Physical Challenge:** Test your endurance on steep trails and high altitudes.

## What to Bring

- Sturdy hiking boots
- Warm clothing (layers are best)
- Sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen)
- Water bottle and purification tablets
- Camera to capture the memories
