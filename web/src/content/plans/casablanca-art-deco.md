## Casablanca's Art Deco Heritage

Step back in time and explore the architectural wonders of Casablanca. This walking tour takes you through the city's center, where French Art Deco meets traditional Moroccan craftsmanship (Mauresque style).

### Highlights

*   **Sacred Heart Cathedral**: A monumental white cathedral that dominates the skyline.
*   **Mohammed V Square**: The bustling heart of the city, home to the Wilaya and the Palace of Justice.
*   **Cinema Rialto**: A nostalgic glimpse into the golden age of cinema.

This tour is perfect for architecture lovers and history buffs.
